Chiranjeevi’s Next Movie with Bobby Deol to Begin Shooting After April 11

Published on April 5, 2026 | Entertainment News

The upcoming film of Megastar Chiranjeevi will officially begin with a pooja ceremony on April 11, 2026, followed by regular shooting shortly after.

Megastar Chiranjeevi is once again making headlines as fresh updates about his upcoming film are creating excitement among fans and the film industry. With decades of experience and unmatched popularity, he continues to be a dominant force in Telugu cinema.

His next project, directed by Bobby Kolli, is already generating strong buzz. Fans are eagerly waiting to witness another mass entertainer featuring the legendary actor in a powerful role.

Powerful Combination Raises Expectations

This project marks a reunion between Chiranjeevi and director Bobby Kolli, who previously delivered a blockbuster together. Their combination is known for high-energy storytelling and strong emotional elements.

The inclusion of Bobby Deol adds further excitement. Known for his impactful performances, he is expected to bring intensity and depth to the film. His role is rumored to be strong and possibly negative, which will enhance the overall narrative.

The film is expected to be a commercial entertainer blending action, emotion, and drama. With a wide appeal, it is likely to attract audiences across different regions.

Chiranjeevi’s Continued Legacy

Chiranjeevi has consistently reinvented himself over the years, adapting to modern trends while maintaining his classic charm. His contribution to Indian cinema is immense, and his influence continues to inspire generations.

His films have always set benchmarks in terms of performance, entertainment, and box office success. Even today, he commands a massive fan base and strong openings for his films.

This upcoming project is expected to continue that legacy by presenting him in a fresh avatar while retaining his signature style.

What to Expect

While the story details are still under wraps, early reports suggest a powerful narrative with emotional depth. Themes like family, justice, and revenge may play a key role in the storyline.

The film is expected to feature high production values, strong music, and impressive visuals. The technical team is focused on delivering a grand cinematic experience.

With everything in place, the movie has the potential to become a major blockbuster.

Conclusion

Chiranjeevi’s upcoming film is undoubtedly one of the most anticipated releases of 2026. The official launch on April 11 will kickstart the project, and fans can expect more exciting updates soon.
