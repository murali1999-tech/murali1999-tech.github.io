Chiranjeevi’s Next Movie with Bobby Deol to Begin Shooting After April 11

Published on April 5, 2026 | Entertainment News

Megastar Chiranjeevi is once again in the spotlight as exciting updates about his upcoming film have started circulating across the film industry. Known for his powerful screen presence and unmatched fan following, Chiranjeevi continues to dominate Telugu cinema even after decades of success. His next project, directed by Bobby Kolli, is already creating massive buzz among fans and movie lovers.

According to the latest reports, the film will officially begin with a grand pooja ceremony scheduled for April 11, 2026. Following this traditional launch event, the regular shooting is expected to commence within a few days. The announcement has sparked excitement among fans who are eagerly waiting to see Chiranjeevi back on the big screen in a powerful role.

Powerful Combination Raises Expectations

This film marks another collaboration between Chiranjeevi and director Bobby Kolli, who previously delivered a blockbuster together. Their earlier film was well received by audiences and showcased Chiranjeevi in a mass entertainer role that perfectly matched his style. With this new project, expectations are naturally sky-high.

Adding to the excitement is the inclusion of Bollywood actor Bobby Deol in a significant role. Chiranjeevi has consistently proven his ability to adapt to changing trends in cinema while maintaining his signature style. His recent films have performed well at the box office, reinforcing his position as one of the biggest stars in Indian cinema. His dedication, discipline, and passion for acting continue to inspire millions of fans.

While the makers have not officially revealed the complete storyline, several reports suggest that the movie will focus on a powerful character with emotional depth. It may include themes of family, revenge, and justice, which are commonly loved by audiences.

With a strong cast, experienced director, and Chiranjeevi in the lead, the film has all the ingredients needed for a blockbuster. The combination of action, emotion, and star power is likely to attract audiences from all sections.
