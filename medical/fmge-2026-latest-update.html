Introduction

The Foreign Medical Graduate Examination (FMGE) is a mandatory licensing exam for students who complete MBBS abroad. Conducted by NBEMS (National Board of Examinations), it allows candidates to practice medicine in India after qualifying.

FMGE 2026 Exam Date

FMGE 2026 is expected in July 2026. Students should regularly check the official website: FMGE Official Page for latest announcements and updates.

Important: Missing the exam session can delay your career. Stay updated using official portals only.

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must follow guidelines set by the National Medical Commission (NMC). Ensure your foreign degree is recognized before applying.

  • Indian citizen or OCI
  • Recognized foreign medical qualification
  • Completion of MBBS before deadline

Documents Required

Before applying, download the official FMGE information bulletin: Download Information Bulletin

  • Medical degree certificate
  • Passport
  • Proof of citizenship
  • Internship certificate
Tip: Keep scanned copies ready in advance to avoid last-minute issues.

Exam Pattern

FMGE is a computer-based test. For detailed syllabus, visit: NBEMS Syllabus Section

Career Opportunities

After clearing FMGE, candidates can register and practice in India or pursue postgraduate courses like NEET PG.

Conclusion

FMGE 2026 is a major milestone for foreign medical graduates. Stay updated, prepare well, and use official resources regularly.
