🔥 Revolt of 1857 - MCQ Quiz

ICSE Class 10 History Practice Test

1. When did the Revolt of 1857 begin?



Correct Answer: 10 May 1857

2. What was the immediate cause of the revolt?



Correct Answer: Enfield cartridges greased with cow and pig fat

3. Who led the revolt in Kanpur?



Correct Answer: Nana Sahib

4. Rani Lakshmibai was the queen of which place?



Correct Answer: Jhansi

5. Who is known as the first martyr of the revolt?



Correct Answer: Mangal Pandey

6. Who was the Governor-General during the revolt?



Correct Answer: Lord Canning

7. Which policy caused annexation of Indian states?



Correct Answer: Doctrine of Lapse

8. What happened after the revolt?



Correct Answer: Company rule ended

9. One reason for failure of the revolt?



Correct Answer: No leadership unity

10. Where was Bahadur Shah Zafar declared Emperor?



Correct Answer: Delhi
