Latest Update

The Andhra Pradesh government has officially clarified that the AP SSC (Class 10) Results 2026 are not being released today. This announcement comes after several fake messages circulated widely on social media platforms, creating confusion among students and parents.

Officials have clearly stated that students should rely only on trusted and official sources for updates regarding the results.

Fake News Alert

In recent days, many students received messages claiming that results would be declared on a specific date. However, these messages were not verified and caused unnecessary panic.

Authorities have warned students not to believe rumors shared on WhatsApp, Telegram, or other social media platforms. Sharing false information can create stress and confusion during an already important time.

Students are advised to double-check any information before trusting it.

Where to Check Results

Once officially announced, students can check their results on the following trusted websites:

Official AP Board Website

AP Results Portal

Students should keep their hall ticket number ready to access their marks quickly when results are released.

Expected Result Date

Although the exact date has not been confirmed yet, results are expected to be released soon. Based on previous years, the announcement usually happens in the last week of April or early May.

Students are advised to stay calm and wait for the official notification instead of relying on rumors.

Importance of SSC Results

The SSC results play a crucial role in a student’s academic journey. These marks determine eligibility for intermediate courses and future career paths.

Students often choose streams like science, commerce, or arts based on their performance. Therefore, it is important to approach this phase with patience and a positive mindset.

Tips for Students While Waiting

Waiting for results can be stressful, but students can use this time productively. Focus on planning your next academic step and exploring career options.

Engage in hobbies, learn new skills, or spend time with family to stay relaxed. Avoid constantly checking for updates, as this may increase anxiety.
