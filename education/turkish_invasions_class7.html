🏰 The Turkish Invasions of India

Class 7 ICSE History & Civics Study Notes

🌍 Introduction

The Turks were Central Asian warriors who invaded India for wealth, power, and empire building. Their invasions changed Indian history significantly.

🗡️ Mahmud of Ghazni

Mahmud of Ghazni invaded India 17 times mainly to loot wealth. He attacked many rich temples.

  • No permanent rule
  • Main aim: wealth
  • Destroyed temples

🏛️ Famous attack: Somnath Temple

⚔️ Muhammad Ghori

Muhammad Ghori wanted to establish permanent rule in India. He defeated Prithviraj Chauhan in the Second Battle of Tarain (1192).

👑 Prithviraj Chauhan

🏛️ Delhi Sultanate Begins

After Ghori’s death, Qutb-ud-din Aibak became the first ruler of the Delhi Sultanate in 1206 AD.

He started building the famous Qutub Minar.

📊 Effects of Turkish Invasions

👍 Positive Effects

  • New architecture (domes, arches)
  • Cultural exchange
  • Better administration

👎 Negative Effects

  • Temple destruction
  • Loss of wealth
  • Political instability
