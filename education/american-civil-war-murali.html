🇺🇸 American Civil War Mind Map

1861 – 1865 | North (Union) vs South (Confederacy)

⚖️ Causes

  • Slavery conflict
  • North vs South economy
  • States’ rights vs federal power
  • Election of Abraham Lincoln (1860)
  • Southern secession

⚔️ Major Events

  • Fort Sumter – war begins
  • Antietam – bloodiest battle
  • Gettysburg – turning point
  • Sherman’s March – destruction in South
  • Appomattox surrender (1865)

🏁 Results

  • Slavery abolished
  • United States preserved
  • Strong federal government
  • Reconstruction begins
  • 600,000+ deaths

🧠 Impact

  • End of slavery system
  • Foundation of civil rights struggle
  • Social change in South
  • Industrial growth in North
  • Modern nation formation
