🇺🇸 American Civil War (1861–1865)

ICSE Class 8 Complete Smart Notes

📌 Introduction

The American Civil War was fought between the Northern states (Union) and Southern states (Confederacy) from 1861 to 1865.

Main issue: Slavery and States’ Rights

⚖️ Causes of War

  • Slavery conflict (main cause)
  • North = industry, South = agriculture
  • States wanted independence from federal control
  • Election of Abraham Lincoln (1860)
  • Southern states formed Confederacy

⚔️ Major Events

  • Fort Sumter – war begins (1861)
  • Antietam – bloodiest battle
  • Gettysburg – turning point
  • Sherman’s March – destruction of South
  • Appomattox – war ends (1865)

🧠 Advanced Key Points

  • North = industrial economy, South = plantation economy
  • War = moral vs economic conflict
  • First modern war (railways, telegraph, rifles)
  • Geography helped North win
  • South collapsed economically during war

🏁 Results & Impact

  • Slavery abolished (13th Amendment)
  • USA remained united
  • Federal government became stronger
  • 600,000+ deaths
  • Reconstruction period began

📜 Important Amendments

  • 13th – abolished slavery
  • 14th – citizenship rights
  • 15th – voting rights to Black men

📚 Conclusion

The American Civil War transformed the USA from a divided nation into a strong unified country. It ended slavery and strengthened federal authority.
