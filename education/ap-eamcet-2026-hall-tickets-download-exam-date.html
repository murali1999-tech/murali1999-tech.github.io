The Andhra Pradesh Engineering, Agriculture, and Medical Common Entrance Test (AP EAMCET) 2026 is one of the most important entrance exams for students aiming to secure admission into professional courses. The latest update confirms that hall tickets will be available for download starting tomorrow, and the examination will commence from May 12, 2026.

Hall Tickets Available from Tomorrow

Candidates who have successfully registered for AP EAMCET 2026 can download their hall tickets from the official website starting tomorrow. The hall ticket is a mandatory document required to appear for the examination. Without it, candidates will not be allowed to enter the examination hall.

Students are advised to download their admit cards as early as possible to avoid last-minute technical issues. It is also recommended to verify all details mentioned on the hall ticket carefully.

Steps to Download AP EAMCET 2026 Hall Ticket

  • Visit the official AP EAMCET website
  • Click on the “Download Hall Ticket” link
  • Enter your registration number, date of birth, and other required details
  • Submit the information
  • Download and print your hall ticket

Exam Dates and Schedule

The AP EAMCET 2026 exams are scheduled to begin from May 12. The exam will be conducted in multiple shifts across various centers in Andhra Pradesh. Candidates should check their specific exam date and time mentioned on the hall ticket.

Important Instructions for Students

  • Carry a printed copy of the hall ticket to the exam center
  • Bring a valid ID proof such as Aadhaar card or school ID
  • Reach the exam center at least one hour before the reporting time
  • Avoid carrying electronic gadgets like mobile phones
  • Follow all instructions given by the invigilators

Details Mentioned on Hall Ticket

The hall ticket will contain important information such as:

  • Candidate’s name and photograph
  • Roll number
  • Exam center details
  • Date and time of examination
  • Important guidelines

Why AP EAMCET is Important

AP EAMCET is a gateway for students to get admission into top engineering, agriculture, and pharmacy colleges in Andhra Pradesh. Thousands of students appear for this exam every year, making it highly competitive.

Preparation Tips

With the exam approaching, students should focus on revision and practice. Solving previous years’ question papers and taking mock tests can help improve performance. Time management is also crucial during the exam.
