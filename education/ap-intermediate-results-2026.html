1st Year & 2nd Year Results Releasing Tomorrow

Latest Update

The Andhra Pradesh Board of Intermediate Education (BIEAP) is expected to release the Intermediate 1st Year and 2nd Year results tomorrow. Lakhs of students across the state are eagerly waiting for their marks.

The results will be available online through official websites. Students are advised to keep their Hall Ticket Number ready to check results quickly.

Official Result Links

You can check your results using the following official websites:

  • https://bie.ap.gov.in
  • https://results.bie.ap.gov.in
  • https://examresults.ap.nic.in

Click below for direct access:

Check Results Now

How to Check AP Intermediate Results

  1. Go to the official website.
  2. Click on "Intermediate Results 2026".
  3. Select 1st Year or 2nd Year.
  4. Enter your Hall Ticket Number.
  5. Click on Submit.
  6. Your result will be displayed on screen.
  7. Download or take a printout for future use.

Details Mentioned in Result

  • Student Name
  • Hall Ticket Number
  • Subject-wise Marks
  • Total Marks
  • Grade
  • Result Status (Pass/Fail)

Important Tips

Due to heavy traffic, official websites may be slow. If the page does not load, wait for a few minutes and try again.

You can also check results through alternative websites or mobile apps if provided by the board.

Always verify your marks with the official marks memo later.

What After Results?

After checking results, students can plan their next academic step. Those who pass can apply for degree courses, engineering, medical, or other professional programs.

If any student is not satisfied with their marks, they can apply for revaluation or recounting.

Students who did not pass can appear for supplementary exams.

Stay Updated

Keep checking official websites and news updates for the exact result release time.
