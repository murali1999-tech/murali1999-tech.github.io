Overview

The NIFT Entrance Exam 2026 Stage 2 is a crucial step for students aiming to join the National Institute of Fashion Technology. This stage evaluates creativity, design skills, and practical knowledge required for fashion-related courses.

Thousands of students across India are appearing for this examination today. With admit cards already released online, candidates must ensure they follow all instructions carefully to avoid any issues at the exam center.

Admit Card Details

The admit card is a mandatory document for entry into the examination hall. Students can download it from the official NIFT website.

Visit Official NIFT Website

It contains important details such as exam center, timing, roll number, and candidate information. Students should verify all details and carry a printed copy along with a valid ID proof.

Exam Guidelines

Students must follow strict guidelines during the examination. Reporting to the center early is highly recommended to avoid last-minute stress.

Electronic devices like mobile phones, smartwatches, and calculators are not allowed. Candidates should carry only essential items such as admit card, ID proof, and required stationery.

Following instructions given by invigilators is very important to maintain discipline and fairness during the exam.

What Stage 2 Includes

Stage 2 of the NIFT exam generally includes tests like Situation Test or Studio Test depending on the course. These tests assess creativity, material handling, and innovative thinking.

Students may be asked to create models or designs using provided materials. Presentation skills and explanation of ideas also play an important role in scoring.

Preparation Tips

Although the exam is already happening, these tips are useful for last-minute revision and future aspirants:

Stay calm and confident during the test. Manage your time effectively and focus on creativity rather than perfection. Read instructions carefully before starting any task.

Practicing design concepts and improving observation skills can help students perform better in such exams.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Many students lose marks due to simple mistakes. Avoid rushing through questions or ignoring instructions. Ensure your work is neat and properly presented.

Do not forget to label your work or explain your design clearly if required. These small details can make a big difference in your final score.
