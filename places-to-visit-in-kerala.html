Best Places to Visit in Kerala

Kerala is one of the most beautiful states in India, known for its green hills, peaceful backwaters, beaches, waterfalls, wildlife, and culture. Here are some of the top places you should visit in Kerala.

Munnar

Munnar is one of the most famous hill stations in Kerala. It is filled with tea plantations, cool weather, misty mountains, and beautiful viewpoints.

Popular attractions include Echo Point, Mattupetty Dam, Eravikulam National Park, and Top Station.

Best time to visit: September to March

Alleppey

Alleppey is famous for its backwaters and houseboats. Visitors can enjoy peaceful boat rides through canals, lakes, and villages.

It is one of the best places in Kerala for couples and families looking for a relaxing vacation.

Best time to visit: October to February

Wayanad

Wayanad is known for forests, waterfalls, caves, wildlife, and scenic mountain views. It is perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Major attractions include Edakkal Caves, Soochipara Falls, Banasura Sagar Dam, and wildlife sanctuaries.

Best time to visit: October to May

Kochi

Kochi is a vibrant city that combines history, culture, shopping, and food. Fort Kochi is especially famous for its colonial architecture and Chinese fishing nets.

You can also visit Marine Drive, Mattancherry Palace, Jew Town, and local spice markets.

Best time to visit: October to February

Thekkady

Thekkady is famous for wildlife and spice plantations. Visitors can enjoy jungle safaris, elephant rides, bamboo rafting, and boating.

The main attraction is Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary where you may spot elephants, deer, and birds.

Best time to visit: September to March

Varkala

Varkala is one of Kerala's most beautiful beach destinations. It is known for cliffs, beach cafes, sunsets, and peaceful surroundings.

Varkala Beach is perfect for relaxing, swimming, and enjoying seafood.

Best time to visit: October to March

Athirappilly Falls

Athirappilly Falls is often called the Niagara Falls of India. It is one of the largest waterfalls in Kerala and a favorite place for tourists and photographers.

The surrounding forest area adds even more beauty to the experience.

Best time to visit: June to September

Kovalam

Kovalam is a popular beach town known for its clean beaches, lighthouse, and water sports. It is a great destination for beach lovers.

Tourists can enjoy surfing, swimming, parasailing, and relaxing by the sea.

Best time to visit: November to February
