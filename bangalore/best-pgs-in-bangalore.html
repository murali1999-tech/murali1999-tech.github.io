Best PGs in Bangalore

Bangalore offers many PG options for students and working professionals. Areas like Koramangala, Whitefield, HSR Layout, and Jayanagar are especially popular because they are close to colleges, offices, restaurants, and metro stations.

Modern Deluxe PG for Gents

Located in Koramangala, this PG is known for clean rooms, good food, strong Wi-Fi, and modern interiors. It is popular among students and IT employees because of its location and comfortable facilities.

Sri Sravya PG for Gents and Ladies

This Whitefield PG is suitable for both men and women. It is close to IT parks and offers furnished rooms, meals, laundry services, and internet access for working professionals.

Zolo Lance

Zolo Lance in HSR Layout is a good choice for both ladies and gents. It provides comfortable rooms, security, housekeeping, food, and app-based support services for residents.

Aashirwad Ladies PG

Located in Jayanagar, this PG is popular among female students and professionals. It is known for safety, clean rooms, good food, and its convenient location near hospitals and shopping areas.

SAI RAM PG FOR GENTS

This PG in Koramangala is highly rated for its affordable rent, good maintenance, and calm atmosphere. It is ideal for working professionals and students who want a budget-friendly stay.

Universe Luxury PG for Ladies

This ladies PG in Koramangala offers modern rooms, attached bathrooms, security, food, and Wi-Fi. It is a good option for women looking for a comfortable and premium stay experience.

Tips Before Choosing a PG

Always check room cleanliness, food quality, Wi-Fi speed, security, and distance from your office or college. Ask about extra charges for electricity, laundry, and deposits before making a final decision.
