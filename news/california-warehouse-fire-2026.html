Massive Warehouse Fire in California

A major fire broke out at a Kimberly-Clark warehouse in Ontario, California, becoming one of the biggest incidents today. Authorities confirmed the fire was intentionally set, and a suspect has been arrested. Around 175 firefighters worked tirelessly to control the blaze, which caused severe structural damage to the building. Despite the intensity of the fire, no casualties were reported. Investigations are ongoing as officials work to determine the full circumstances behind the incident and assess the total extent of damage caused.
