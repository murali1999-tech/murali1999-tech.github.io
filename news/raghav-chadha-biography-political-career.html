Raghav Chadha is one of the youngest and most popular politicians in India today. He is a senior leader of the Aam Aadmi Party and has become widely known for his speeches, debates, and role in Parliament. Over the years, he has gained popularity among young voters because of his calm speaking style and strong political presence.

Early Life and Education

Raghav Chadha was born on November 11, 1988, in New Delhi. He completed his education in Delhi and later studied commerce at the University of Delhi. He also became a chartered accountant by studying at the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India.

To improve his knowledge in finance and economics, he later attended the London School of Economics. Before joining politics, he worked with well-known companies such as Deloitte and Grant Thornton.

Political Journey

Raghav Chadha entered public life during the anti-corruption movement led by Arvind Kejriwal in 2011. He quickly became one of the most visible faces of the movement and later joined the Aam Aadmi Party.

As the party grew, Raghav Chadha became an important spokesperson and leader. In 2020, he was elected as an MLA from Rajinder Nagar in Delhi. Two years later, he became a Rajya Sabha Member of Parliament from Punjab.

His speeches in Parliament and on television debates helped him become one of the best-known young leaders in the country. Many people see him as one of the future faces of AAP.

Personal Life

Raghav Chadha married actress Parineeti Chopra in 2023. Their wedding received huge media attention because it brought together politics and the entertainment industry. In 2025, reports said that the couple welcomed their first child, a son.

Recent News

In recent weeks, Raghav Chadha has been in the news because of growing tensions inside the Aam Aadmi Party. He was removed from the post of deputy leader in the Rajya Sabha, which led to rumors about his future role in the party.

Some reports suggest that there may be disagreements between him and senior party leaders. Because of this, many political observers are now closely watching what he does next in his political career.
