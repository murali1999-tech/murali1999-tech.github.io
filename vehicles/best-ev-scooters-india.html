Best EV Scooters in India

Electric scooters are becoming more popular because they are affordable, eco-friendly, and cheaper to maintain than petrol scooters. Here are some of the best EV scooters in India in 2026.

Ola S1 Pro

Ola S1 Pro is one of the most popular electric scooters in India. It offers strong performance, a stylish design, a large touchscreen display, and long range. It is a great option for city riders and daily commuting.

Ather 450X

Ather 450X is known for its sporty performance and premium build quality. It comes with smart features like navigation, ride statistics, and fast charging. The scooter is ideal for tech lovers who want a modern EV experience.

TVS iQube

TVS iQube is one of the most practical electric scooters for Indian roads. It offers comfortable seating, smooth ride quality, decent range, and trusted service support from TVS.

Bajaj Chetak EV

Bajaj Chetak EV combines retro design with modern electric technology. It is stylish, premium, and built with strong metal body panels. It is a good choice for people who want a classy electric scooter.

Vida V1

Vida V1 is a feature-rich electric scooter from Hero. It comes with removable batteries, a smart dashboard, and good performance. It is useful for people who may not have charging points at home.

Simple One

Simple One offers one of the longest ranges among electric scooters in India. It has a stylish look, large storage space, and modern features. It is suitable for people who travel longer distances daily.

Tips Before Buying an EV Scooter

Always check the charging time, range, battery warranty, service network, and resale value before buying an electric scooter. Compare monthly running costs with petrol scooters to see how much money you can save in the long run.
