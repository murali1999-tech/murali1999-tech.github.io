EV Subsidies in India

home about contact us Privacy

The Indian government offers subsidies on electric scooters and cars to encourage more people to switch to EVs. Buyers can get lower prices through central schemes and additional state subsidies. Some states also offer road tax exemptions, registration fee waivers, and discounts on charging equipment. These benefits help reduce the total cost of owning an electric vehicle.
