Habit Tracker – Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Habit Tracker collects, uses, and protects your information while using the app.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data from you. Habit Tracker only stores:

  • Your habits (name, goals, reminder time).
  • Habit completion status and streaks.
  • Settings like dark mode preference.

All data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences.

3. How We Use Your Data

All stored data is used solely to provide you with habit tracking features, reminders, and progress tracking. We do not share your data with any third parties.

4. Notifications

Habit Tracker uses local notifications to remind you of your habits. Notifications are generated on your device and do not require internet access.

5. Security

Your data is stored securely on your device. Habit Tracker does not transmit your data to servers or cloud services.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
