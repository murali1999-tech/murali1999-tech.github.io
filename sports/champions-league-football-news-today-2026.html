🏆 FIFA World Cup 2026 Big Update

FIFA has confirmed a historic change for the 2026 World Cup final. For the first time ever, a halftime entertainment show will be included, similar to the Super Bowl. Music direction will be led by Chris Martin, bringing a global entertainment experience to football’s biggest stage. This marks a major shift in how FIFA presents its final match.
