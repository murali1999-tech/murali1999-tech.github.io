Andhra Pradesh 10th Class Exam Updates 2026

The AP SSC Board has announced that due to certain mistakes in the English and Hindi question papers, additional marks will be awarded to students. This decision applies only if the students attempted the affected questions, even if the answers were incorrect.

Specifically:

  • English: 4 marks will be added. Question 28, which required writing antonyms for underlined words from the workbook (not the textbook), will now be awarded marks for all students who attempted it.
  • Hindi: 3 marks will be added for questions where errors occurred in 3–6 bits of the paper.

The evaluation of Class 10 papers will begin on 6th April 2026 and continue until 15th April 2026. Processing the results will take approximately 8–10 days. The tentative date for the announcement of results is 25th April 2026.

This information was provided by the Government Exam Inspector, Srinivasulu Reddy.

Students are advised to stay updated and check the official SSC website for further announcements.
