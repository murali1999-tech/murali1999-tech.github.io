About Daily Sloka

Daily Sloka is a simple Android app made for people who want to read and understand spiritual slokas every day. The app contains meaningful slokas inspired by Ramayana, Mahabharata, and Bhagavatam. Each sloka comes with English meaning and తెలుగు అర్థం so users can understand the message clearly.

The app is lightweight, fast, and easy to use. Users can copy slokas instantly, share them with friends and family, and build a daily habit of devotion and positivity. Daily Sloka is suitable for all age groups and does not collect personal data.
