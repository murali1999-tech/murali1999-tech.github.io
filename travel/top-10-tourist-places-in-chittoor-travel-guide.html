4. Kanipakam Temple

Famous for Lord Ganesha, Kanipakam Temple is known for its unique self-manifested idol and strong spiritual belief among devotees.

5. Chandragiri Fort

This historical fort reflects the rich history of the Vijayanagara Empire. It is a must-visit for history lovers.

6. Kailasakona Waterfalls

A peaceful waterfall spot known for its natural beauty and temple nearby.

7. Sri Kalahasti Temple

One of the famous Shiva temples in South India, known for Rahu-Ketu pooja.

8. Nagalapuram

A trekking paradise with waterfalls and adventure spots.

9. Kaundinya Wildlife Sanctuary

Known for elephants and wildlife, this sanctuary is perfect for nature and wildlife lovers.

10. Gudimallam Temple

One of the oldest Shiva temples in India with historical significance.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Chittoor is between October and March when the weather is pleasant and suitable for travel.

Travel Tips

  • Carry water and essentials during summer
  • Book Tirupati darshan tickets in advance
  • Wear comfortable clothing for trekking
  • Start early to avoid crowds

Budget Guide

A trip to Chittoor can be budget-friendly. Average cost:

  • Travel: ₹500 – ₹2000
  • Food: ₹300 – ₹800 per day
  • Stay: ₹800 – ₹3000 per night
