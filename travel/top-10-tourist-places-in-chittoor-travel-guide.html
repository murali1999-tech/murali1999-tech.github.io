Top 10 Tourist Places in Chittoor You Must Visit

Chittoor is one of the most beautiful and culturally rich districts in Andhra Pradesh. From spiritual destinations to scenic hill stations and waterfalls, Chittoor offers a perfect mix for travelers.

1. Tirupati

Tirupati is one of the most visited pilgrimage destinations in the world. The famous Tirumala Tirupati Temple attracts millions of devotees every year.

2. Horsley Hills

Horsley Hills is a perfect getaway for nature lovers with cool weather and scenic beauty.

3. Talakona Waterfalls

The highest waterfall in Andhra Pradesh, ideal for trekking and nature exploration.

4. Kanipakam Temple

Famous for Lord Ganesha and spiritual beliefs.

5. Chandragiri Fort

A historical fort from the Vijayanagara Empire.

6. Kailasakona Waterfalls

A peaceful waterfall surrounded by greenery.

7. Sri Kalahasti Temple

Famous Shiva temple known for Rahu-Ketu pooja.

8. Nagalapuram

Popular trekking destination with waterfalls.

9. Kaundinya Wildlife Sanctuary

Known for elephants and wildlife lovers.

10. Gudimallam Temple

One of the oldest Shiva temples in India.

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best time.

Travel Tips

  • Book Tirupati tickets early
  • Carry water in summer
  • Wear comfortable clothes

Budget Guide

₹1000–₹3000 per day is enough for a normal trip.
