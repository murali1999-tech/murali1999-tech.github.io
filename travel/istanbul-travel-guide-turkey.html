Introduction

Istanbul, the largest city in Turkey, is a fascinating destination that blends rich history with modern life. It is the only city in the world that spans two continents — Europe and Asia — making it truly unique.

Top Attractions

  • Hagia Sophia – A masterpiece of architecture and history
  • Blue Mosque – Famous for its stunning blue tiles
  • Grand Bazaar – One of the oldest and largest markets in the world
  • Bosphorus Cruise – A beautiful journey between two continents

Food Experience

Istanbul offers a rich variety of delicious food including kebabs, baklava, Turkish tea, and street foods like simit. The flavors reflect a mix of cultures and traditions.

Travel Tips

  • Carry Turkish Lira for local shopping
  • Use public transport like trams and metro
  • Respect local culture and dress modestly at religious places
  • Visit early morning to avoid crowds

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Istanbul is during spring (April to June) and autumn (September to November) when the weather is pleasant.
