Top Beaches in India to Visit in 2026

India is home to some of the most beautiful beaches in the world. From lively party beaches in Goa to peaceful shores in Kerala and the Andaman Islands, there is something for everyone in 2026.

Goa

Goa remains the most famous beach destination in India. It is known for nightlife, beach shacks, water sports, parties, seafood, and beautiful sunsets. Beaches like Baga, Calangute, Vagator, and Palolem are among the most visited.

North Goa is perfect for nightlife and fun, while South Goa offers a more peaceful atmosphere with luxury resorts and quiet beaches.

Best Time to Visit: November to February

Radhanagar Beach, Andaman

Radhanagar Beach on Havelock Island is often ranked among the best beaches in Asia. It is famous for crystal-clear water, white sand, and beautiful sunsets.

This beach is ideal for couples, families, and photographers who want to enjoy nature away from crowded places.

Best Time to Visit: October to May

Kovalam, Kerala

Kovalam is one of Kerala's top beach destinations. It is known for its lighthouse, soft sand, palm trees, and calm atmosphere. Visitors can enjoy surfing, swimming, and relaxing by the sea.

It is also a popular place for Ayurvedic treatments and wellness tourism.

Best Time to Visit: November to February

Varkala Beach, Kerala

Varkala is one of the most unique beaches in India because of its dramatic cliffs overlooking the sea. The beach is lined with cafes, shops, and beautiful viewpoints.

It is perfect for travelers looking for a mix of beach life, yoga, relaxation, and scenic beauty.

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Gokarna, Karnataka

Gokarna has become very popular among young travelers who want a quieter alternative to Goa. Beaches like Om Beach, Kudle Beach, and Paradise Beach offer stunning views and a peaceful environment.

It is a great place for trekking, beach camping, and watching sunsets.

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Marina Beach, Chennai

Marina Beach is one of the longest urban beaches in the world. Located in Chennai, it attracts thousands of visitors every day.

The beach is famous for evening walks, local snacks, horse rides, and sunrise views.

Best Time to Visit: November to February

Puri Beach, Odisha

Puri Beach is a beautiful destination on the east coast of India. It is known for its golden sand, local culture, and proximity to the famous Jagannath Temple.

Many travelers combine their beach visit with temple tourism and seafood experiences.

Best Time to Visit: October to February

Pondicherry Beaches

Pondicherry is known for clean beaches, French-style streets, cafes, and peaceful vibes. Popular beaches include Paradise Beach, Serenity Beach, and Promenade Beach.

It is an ideal destination for people looking for a mix of beach relaxation and city charm.

Best Time to Visit: October to March
