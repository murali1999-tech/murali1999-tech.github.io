Top Places to Visit Near Hyderabad

Looking for a quick weekend getaway from Hyderabad? From waterfalls and hills to forts, temples, and lakes, there are many beautiful places around Hyderabad perfect for a short trip.

Ananthagiri Hills

Ananthagiri Hills is one of the closest hill stations near Hyderabad and is perfect for nature lovers. Located around 80 kilometers away, this place is known for green forests, scenic viewpoints, coffee plantations, and pleasant weather. Trekking enthusiasts love this destination because of its peaceful walking trails.

Nagarjuna Sagar

Nagarjuna Sagar is famous for its massive dam built across the Krishna River. It is located around 165 kilometers from Hyderabad and offers beautiful views, boat rides, and nearby attractions like Nagarjunakonda Island. The place becomes even more attractive during the monsoon season.

Warangal

Warangal is a historical city known for ancient forts, temples, and lakes. It is home to famous places like Warangal Fort, Thousand Pillar Temple, and Bhadrakali Lake. Visitors interested in history and architecture will enjoy exploring this beautiful destination.

Bidar

Located in Karnataka, Bidar is known for its magnificent fort, Bahmani architecture, and old-world charm. Bidar Fort, Gurudwara Nanak Jhira Sahib, and Bahmani Tombs are among the top attractions. It is a great destination for a one-day or weekend trip.

Srisailam

Srisailam is a popular spiritual destination located in the Nallamala Hills. It is famous for the Mallikarjuna Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas in India. The scenic roads, forest views, dam, and wildlife sanctuary make Srisailam a wonderful place for both pilgrims and nature lovers.

Ramoji Film City

Ramoji Film City is one of the most famous attractions near Hyderabad. It is one of the largest film studio complexes in the world and offers movie sets, gardens, amusement rides, and entertainment shows. Families and children especially enjoy spending a full day here.

Medak Fort and Church

Medak is known for its historic fort and one of the largest churches in India. The Medak Cathedral is a major attraction with beautiful architecture. The fort offers great panoramic views of the surrounding region and is a good choice for a peaceful road trip.

Travel Tips for Weekend Trips

Start early in the morning to avoid traffic while leaving Hyderabad. Carry water bottles, snacks, comfortable footwear, and a power bank. If you are visiting during monsoon, take umbrellas or raincoats and check weather conditions before leaving.
