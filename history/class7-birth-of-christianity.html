🌟 The Birth of Christianity – Summary

By Muralidhar.C

📍 1. Background

  • Christianity began in Palestine, part of the Roman Empire.
  • The people were mostly Jews waiting for a Messiah.

👶 2. Birth of Jesus Christ

  • Jesus Christ was born in Bethlehem.
  • His mother was Mary and foster father Joseph.
  • His birth is celebrated as Christmas.

📖 3. Teachings of Jesus

  • Believe in one God
  • Love all people
  • Forgive others
  • Be kind and truthful
  • Help the poor

Message: Love, peace, brotherhood

⚖️ 4. Opposition and Crucifixion

  • Opposed by religious leaders
  • Sentenced by Pontius Pilate
  • Crucified at Golgotha

✝️ 5. Resurrection

  • Jesus rose after three days
  • Celebrated as Easter

🌍 6. Spread of Christianity

  • Spread by disciples
  • Saint Peter and Saint Paul played key roles

📚 7. The Bible

  • Holy book of Christians
  • Old Testament and New Testament

⛪ 8. Early Persecution

  • Christians were persecuted
  • Refused emperor worship
  • Many were killed

👑 9. Acceptance

  • Constantine allowed religious freedom (313 AD)
  • Christianity became state religion

🌍 10. Growth of Church

  • Churches built
  • Pope became leader

📜 11. Importance

  • Influenced laws and culture
  • Promoted equality and charity

📖 12. Teachings in Bible

  • Recorded in New Testament
  • Includes parables

🙏 13. Moral Values

  • Love, forgiveness, service, equality

🌐 14. Christianity Today

  • One of the largest religions
  • Festivals: Christmas, Easter

✝️ 15. Crucifixion

  • Roman method of execution
  • Jesus crucified at Golgotha
  • Remembered on Good Friday

🌅 16. Resurrection

  • Jesus rose after 3 days
  • Strengthened followers' faith

📢 17. Spread After Resurrection

  • Disciples spread teachings
  • Christianity spread widely

🌍 18. Growth

  • Became major religion
  • Attracted many followers

⛪ 19. Formation of Church

  • Churches established
  • Priests and bishops guided people

👑 20. State Religion

  • Supported by Constantine
  • Spread rapidly

📚 21. Role of Church

  • Influenced society and politics
  • Helped poor and educated people

🌐 22. Modern Christianity

  • Followed worldwide
  • Teaches love and peace

📖 23. Lasting Impact

  • Promoted compassion and equality

🏫 24. Contribution

  • Schools, hospitals, charity

🌍 25. World Religion

  • Spread globally
  • Guides millions

⛪ 26. Branches

  • Roman Catholic
  • Protestant
  • Orthodox

📜 27. Moral Teachings Today

  • Helping others, truth, peace

🤝 28. Brotherhood

  • All humans equal
  • Promotes unity

🌟 29. Relevance

  • Important for modern society

🎯 Final Conclusion

Christianity teaches love, forgiveness, and equality.
